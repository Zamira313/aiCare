Internal Helpdesk Automation
with aiCare
In large organizations, managing internal support requests—whether IT-related issues, HR inquiries, or operational tasks—can be a daunting challenge. aiCare’s Internal Helpdesk Automation is designed to streamline the handling of these support requests, ensuring employees receive fast, efficient service across multiple channels. What makes aiCare’s solution unique is its ability to offer secure, role-based access, ensuring sensitive data is only shared with authorized users. By identifying users based on their communication channel and assigned role, aiCare tailors responses and restricts data access accordingly, providing a secure, efficient, and personalized internal support system.
Key features
Key Features of Internal
Helpdesk Automation with
aiCare
Multi-Channel Support

Multi-Channel Support for Employee Inquiries

aiCare provides omni-channel support, allowing employees to submit IT, HR, or operational support requests through a wide range of communication platforms such as WhatsApp, Slack, Microsoft Teams, email, or your company’s intranet. Employees can engage with aiCare in the same way they would a live support agent, receiving real-time, AI-driven assistance to resolve issues.

For example, an employee can message aiCare via Slack to reset their password or inquire about company leave policies, and aiCare will immediately respond with the relevant information or instructions.

Secure User Id

Secure User Identification and Role-Based Permissions

Security is a critical aspect of internal helpdesk automation, especially when sensitive data such as personal employee information or financial records are involved. aiCare identifies each user based on the channel they are using (e.g., Slack, Teams, email) and verifies their identity through secure authentication mechanisms (e.g., Single Sign-On (SSO) or two-factor authentication).

Once the user is authenticated, aiCare assigns the appropriate role-based permissions to the bot, ensuring that only authorized users can access specific types of data. For instance, an HR representative may have access to employee files, while a standard employee can only view basic policies or their personal leave balances.

Automated Support

Automated IT and HR Support

aiCare automates common IT and HR tasks, such as password resets, account provisioning, software access requests, payroll inquiries, and leave management. By providing instant, AI-driven responses, aiCare helps employees resolve their issues quickly, without waiting for human intervention.

For example, an employee facing an issue with email access can contact aiCare for a password reset or troubleshooting steps. Similarly, HR-related queries, such as requesting payslips or checking benefits eligibility, can be handled automatically.

Context-Aware Responses

Context-Aware Responses

aiCare ensures that responses are context-aware, based on the user’s role and specific situation. For instance, when an employee asks for time-off policies, aiCare checks the employee’s department and role to provide relevant policies that apply to them, ensuring accurate and personalized information is delivered.

Additionally, aiCare uses the user’s previous interactions and queries to better understand their needs and provide more efficient support over time.

Role-Based Access

Role-Based Access to Sensitive Data

aiCare’s role-based permission system allows the bot to tailor responses and control access to sensitive data based on the employee’s position and department. For instance, a manager might have the ability to view team members’ performance reports or approve leave requests, while a standard employee can only access their personal information or make requests related to their own employment.

By integrating with the company’s HR management systems (HRMS) or identity management tools, aiCare ensures that access is granted dynamically based on real-time user roles, providing a secure and compliant helpdesk experience.

Human Escalation

Human Escalation for Complex Requests

While aiCare can handle the majority of routine helpdesk requests, it also has the capability to escalate more complex issues to human agents when necessary. For example, if an employee submits a request that requires managerial approval or involves a technical issue that requires hands-on troubleshooting, aiCare seamlessly transfers the case to the appropriate department with all relevant information.

The system ensures that human agents can pick up where aiCare left off, with no need for the employee to repeat information.

Automated Tracking

Automated Ticket Creation and Tracking

aiCare can automatically create support tickets for issues that require further action or human intervention. These tickets are logged into the company’s ticketing system (e.g., ServiceNow, Zendesk, Jira), and the employee receives regular updates on the status of their request, ensuring transparency and accountability.

For example, if an employee submits a hardware replacement request, aiCare generates a support ticket, tracks the progress, and notifies the employee when the request has been processed.

Integrations

Integration with IT and HR Systems

aiCare integrates seamlessly with your organization’s existing IT, HR, and operations systems, such as Active Directory, ServiceNow, Workday, and other internal databases. This integration ensures that aiCare can access real-time data for employee records, device management, and support processes, making it an indispensable tool for internal helpdesk automation.

For example, when an employee requests a password reset, aiCare communicates directly with the company’s identity management system to execute the reset and confirm completion.

Real-Time Reporting

Real-Time Analytics and Reporting

aiCare provides real-time analytics on employee inquiries, support requests, and system usage. This data allows IT and HR teams to identify common issues, optimize workflows, and anticipate future support needs. Managers can review reports on ticket resolution times, employee satisfaction, and system performance.

For instance, if a high number of employees are requesting access to a particular software tool, the IT team can proactively address this by rolling out the software to relevant departments or users.

Data Security

Data Security and Compliance

aiCare’s robust security protocols ensure that all interactions and data transactions comply with company policies and regulatory requirements, such as GDPR or ISO 27001. By leveraging end-to-end encryption and role-based access controls, aiCare protects sensitive employee information from unauthorized access.

Employees can rest assured that their personal data and support requests are handled in a confidential and secure manner.

Case example
Use Case Example
An employee in the finance department has forgotten their password and needs to reset it to access their company email. The employee sends a message to aiCare through Microsoft Teams, requesting a password reset. aiCare verifies the employee’s identity through the company’s Single Sign-On (SSO) system, checks their role and permissions, and initiates the password reset process.

Once the password is successfully reset, aiCare notifies the employee and logs the action in the company’s ticketing system. If the employee has additional issues, such as needing access to a specific financial software, aiCare can process the request based on the employee’s role. If necessary, the case can be escalated to a human IT agent for further assistance.
