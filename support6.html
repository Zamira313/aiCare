Investor Relations Support
with aiCare
Investor relations (IR) is a critical function for businesses that seek to maintain transparency and build trust with their stakeholders. With the Investor Relations Support feature, aiCare enhances communication between your business and its investors, providing timely, accurate, and personalized information. Whether addressing common inquiries, distributing important financial updates, or guiding investors through critical information, aiCare ensures a seamless and efficient investor relations experience. It is available 24/7, allowing investors to access key information on-demand across multiple communication channels.
Key features
Key Features of Investor
Relations Support with aiCare
Real-time Access

Real-Time Access to Investor Information

aiCare integrates with your internal systems to provide real-time access to important investor-related information. Investors can inquire about financial reports, stock performance, upcoming meetings, and other critical data at any time, and aiCare will deliver accurate, up-to-date responses instantly.

For example, investors can ask, “What were the earnings for Q3?” or “When is the next earnings call?” and aiCare will pull the latest information from your system, ensuring investors stay informed.

24/7 availability

24/7 Availability Across Multiple Channels

aiCare ensures that investors can access the information they need at any time of day or night. Whether they prefer to communicate via WhatsApp, Telegram, Facebook Messenger, LinkedIn, email, or directly through your corporate website, aiCare provides omni-channel support. This makes it easy for investors to reach out and obtain information without waiting for traditional business hours.

By offering continuous support, aiCare helps investors stay informed without requiring human agents, significantly reducing response times.

Personalized interactions

Personalized Investor Interactions

aiCare can personalize interactions based on investor profiles and preferences. By integrating with your CRM or investor management system, aiCare tailors responses according to individual investor needs and provides relevant information based on their relationship with your business.

For example, if an investor frequently inquires about dividend payments, aiCare can recognize this and proactively provide updates or reminders about upcoming payments or changes to dividends, enhancing the overall investor experience.

Distribution of updates

Distribution of Financial Reports and Updates

aiCare can automate the distribution of financial reports, press releases, and other important updates. Investors can request specific documents, such as quarterly earnings reports or annual financial statements, and aiCare will provide direct links or attachments. The system ensures that all investors receive consistent, accurate information.

aiCare can also notify investors when new reports or updates are available, ensuring they stay up-to-date on company performance without having to search for the latest data.

Guiding investors

Guiding Investors Through Complex Information

Investor relations often involve navigating through complex financial documents, regulations, and disclosures. aiCare can help guide investors through complicated information, providing explanations, definitions, and context where needed.

For example, if an investor asks about a specific line item in a financial statement, aiCare can offer a simple explanation, ensuring that even non-experts can understand critical information.

auto scheduling

Automated Meeting Scheduling and Reminders

aiCare can streamline the scheduling of investor meetings, calls, or events. Investors can request meetings or sign up for earnings calls, and aiCare will automatically schedule these in the relevant systems and send reminders.

This automation helps reduce the administrative burden on investor relations teams while ensuring that investors receive timely notifications about upcoming events.

Proactive engagement

Proactive Engagement with Investors

aiCare can be configured to engage proactively with investors, sending personalized messages or notifications about key developments, upcoming events, or potential opportunities to engage with company leadership. This proactive approach fosters stronger relationships and ensures that investors feel valued and informed.

For example, aiCare could send an alert to investors about an upcoming annual shareholder meeting or notify them about recent developments that could affect their investments.

Human escalation

Human Escalation for Critical Issues

While aiCare handles most routine inquiries and interactions, there may be situations where investors need to speak with a human representative. aiCare is equipped with seamless human escalation features, ensuring that critical issues are quickly transferred to an IR professional.

For instance, if an investor has a complex question about the company’s strategy or future outlook, aiCare can escalate the case to the appropriate team member, providing all relevant context to ensure a smooth handoff.

Data security

Data Security and Confidentiality

Investor relations often involve handling sensitive and confidential information. aiCare ensures that all interactions are secure, complying with industry standards and regulations to protect investor data. The platform can be configured to limit access to certain information based on user roles, ensuring that only authorized individuals can access specific documents or details.

For example, general investors may have access to basic financial data, while institutional investors or board members may be granted access to more detailed reports or private communications.

Tracking engagement

Tracking Investor Engagement

aiCare provides valuable insights into investor engagement by tracking interactions, frequently asked questions, and the most requested information. This data allows IR teams to better understand investor priorities, concerns, and engagement levels, enabling more targeted communication strategies.

For example, if aiCare detects that many investors are asking about a particular topic, such as dividend changes, the IR team can prioritize that issue in upcoming communications or address it during earnings calls.

Case example
Use Case Example
An institutional investor reaches out to aiCare via LinkedIn to inquire about the latest quarterly earnings report. aiCare instantly retrieves the report from the company’s investor portal and sends it directly to the investor. During the conversation, the investor asks for clarification on a specific financial metric. aiCare provides a simple, easy-to-understand explanation and offers additional context based on historical performance.

After delivering the requested information, aiCare identifies that the investor frequently inquires about financial metrics and offers to schedule a follow-up call with the company’s CFO to discuss future earnings projections. aiCare handles the meeting scheduling and sends an automated reminder a day before the call, ensuring a smooth and professional interaction.
